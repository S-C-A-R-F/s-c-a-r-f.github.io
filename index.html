About SCARF

Formed in the wake of 2024's race riots, Sheffield Communities Against Racism and Fascism is a collective of concerned citizens seeking to counter the threat of a resurgent and emboldened far right. We do this though building stronger community bonds, promoting positive social change, and confronting racist street movements.

SCARF doesn't seek to dictate tactics or strategy, nor are we interested in accumulating power or influence. We exist to coordinate, network, and mobilize resistance to racism and fascism in Sheffield and the wider area.
